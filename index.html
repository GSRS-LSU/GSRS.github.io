Welcome to EECS GSRS 2020

The fourth EECS Graduate Student Research Symposium (EECS GSRS) will be held on April 9, 2020 in the Commons (Patrick F. Taylor Hall). This an annual research symposium that are organized by the faculty and students of the Electric al Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) school at LSU.

This will be a one-day long symposium where graduate students will have the opportunity to present their research projects and exchange ideas with their peers. Presentations will be in the form of posters.

The students will have the opportunity to interact with faculty as well as their fellow colleagues during this event. The EECS Graduate Student Research Symposium promotes interdisciplinary and collaborative research efforts and spark novel and groundbreaking research ideas. This event is open to public and researchers from other departments and universities are welcome to attend.

Eligibility: The eligibility criteria for students in the division of Computer Science and Engineering are:
  • The applicant MUST be an enrolled graduate student, either MS or PhD
  • The applicant MUST have at least one accepted conference or journal (first author) paper under the LSU affiliation
The eligibility criteria for students in Electrical Engineering are:
  • The applicant MUST be an enrolled graduate student, either MS or PhD
  • The applicant MUST have at least one accepted journal (first author) paper under the LSU affiliation

The eligibility criteria for students in Computer Engineering are:
  • The applicant MUST be enrolled as a graduate student, either MS or PhD
  • The applicant MUST have at least one under review/accepted journal (first author) paper under the LSU affiliation OR one accepted conference (first author) in a venue listed in the attached file.

Application submission deadline: March 13, 2020 @ 5:00pm
